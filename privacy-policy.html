Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2024

Introduction

Time Tracker is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how your information is handled when you use our Chrome extension.

Information Collection and Use

Time Tracker operates entirely on your local machine and does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on external servers. Here's what you should know:

  • All tracking data, including website visit durations and statistics, is stored locally on your device
  • Your preferences and settings are saved locally using Chrome's storage API
  • No personally identifiable information is collected or transmitted
  • No data is shared with third parties

Local Storage

The extension stores the following information locally on your device:

  • Website visit durations and statistics
  • Daily and weekly usage patterns
  • User preferences (such as dark mode settings)
  • Time limit configurations
  • Productivity settings and categories

Payment Processing

For premium features, we use ExtensionPay.com as our payment processor. When making a payment:

  • Payment information is handled directly by ExtensionPay.com
  • We do not store or have access to your payment details
  • ExtensionPay.com's privacy policy applies to payment transactions

Permissions

The extension requires certain permissions to function:

  • Storage: To save your data locally
  • Tabs & Active Tab: To track time spent on websites
  • Notifications: To send time limit alerts
  • Alarms: To manage time tracking intervals
  • Scripting: To monitor website activity

Data Retention and Deletion

All data is stored locally and can be deleted at any time by:

  • Using the clear data option in the extension settings
  • Uninstalling the extension

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy.

Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, you can contact us through the Chrome Web Store developer contact form on our extension page.
