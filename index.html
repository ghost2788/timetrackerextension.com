Frequently Asked Questions

How does Time Tracker protect my privacy?

All your data is stored locally on your device. We don't collect or transmit any of your browsing data to external servers.

Is the Pro version a subscription?

No, the Pro version is a one-time payment of $9.99. Once purchased, you'll have lifetime access to all premium features.

What's included in the free version?

The free version includes basic time tracking, daily statistics, site overview, and custom categories. It's perfect for users who want to understand their browsing habits.

What extra features do I get with Pro?

Pro users get access to weekly analytics reports, set custom goals & time limits and you get access to all future features for free.
